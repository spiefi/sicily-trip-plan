The Route

The good version is western Sicily: Palermo, Scopello or Castellammare, then Trapani, Marsala, or Favignana.

Palermo / Cefalu 1-2 nights. Arrival, markets, food, sea reset.
Scopello / Castellammare 4 nights. Best base for beaches and Zingaro.
Trapani / Marsala 3-4 nights. Favignana, salt pans, wine, sunset dinners.

Important call

Do not sleep in San Vito Lo Capo during these dates. Cous Cous Fest runs 18-27 September 2026. Go one evening for food and atmosphere, then sleep elsewhere where parking and prices are less stupid.

Airport Berlin BER to Palermo PMO is the right pairing.
Fallback Catania works only if Palermo flights become painful.
Tempo Three bases keeps the trip relaxed instead of turning it into a spreadsheet.

Where To Stay

Book the middle base first. The festival overlap will pressure the whole San Vito and Scopello area.

Start

Palermo or Cefalu

One night is enough unless you want city chaos. Do markets, seafood, street food, and a gentle first swim.

Main base

Scopello / Castellammare

The best center of gravity for Zingaro Reserve, Tonnara di Scopello, San Vito, Macari, and slow beach days.

Finish

Trapani / Marsala / Favignana

Pick this for Egadi islands, tuna, salt pans, Marsala wine, and the proper blue-water ending.

Day Sketch

The route has structure, but leaves room to be lazy. Sicily rewards appetite and timing more than heroic sightseeing.

1

Arrive in Palermo

Pick up the car, check in, eat street food, keep the evening easy.

2

Palermo, Mondello, or Cefalu

Markets in the morning, sea in the afternoon, then sleep Palermo or Cefalu.

3

Drive to Scopello

Settle into the main base and swim at Tonnara di Scopello if access works.

4

Zingaro Reserve

Beach hike to Cala Tonnarella dell'Uzzo or Cala dell'Uzzo. Shoes and water.

5

San Vito Lo Capo

Beach day, Cous Cous Fest evening, then drive back out before it gets tedious.

6

Macari Sunset Day

Macari, Bue Marino, slow lunch, no forced itinerary. This is the vacation bit.

7

Trapani or Marsala

Move west, check in, salt pans at sunset, seafood dinner.

8

Favignana

Hydrofoil from Trapani, rent e-bikes, Cala Rossa, Cala Azzurra, Bue Marino.

9

Marsala Buffer Day

Wine, seafood, beach, or another island day if everyone still has legs.

10

Back to Palermo

Drive to PMO, fly home, leave enough margin because car returns love drama.

Food and beaches

  • Palermo: arancine, panelle, sfincione, seafood pasta.
  • San Vito: cous cous during the festival.
  • Trapani: busiate al pesto trapanese.
  • Marsala: seafood, wine, salt-pan aperitivo.
  • Favignana: tuna, granita, Cala Rossa, Cala Azzurra.
